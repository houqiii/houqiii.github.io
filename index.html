About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Lan Zhang. I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Lanzhou University in 2024.

My research centers on reliable intent realization for agents in the physical world — grounding large language models so they can turn high-level human intent into dependable, verifiable actions in real, physical environments.
